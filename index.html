Download & Install

Get Grammar & Spelling Buddy running in under a minute — no account needed.

Download Extension (.zip)

Free · ~100 KB · No sign-up required

1

Download & unzip

Click the button above to download the .zip file. Unzip it anywhere on your computer — you'll see a folder called extension inside.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions in your address bar. Then turn on Developer mode using the toggle in the top-right corner.

3

Load the extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder you unzipped. The purple "G" icon will appear in your toolbar — you're all set!
